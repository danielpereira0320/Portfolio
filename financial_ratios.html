Analysis of Financial Ratios in Predicting Company Bankruptcy

A research project that focuses on the following objectives:

  • Analyzing the role of financial ratios in bankruptcy prediction.
  • Identifying the most effective ratios and their predictive accuracy.
  • Building machine learning models for early-stage prediction.

Key Findings

Significant Financial Ratios Identified:

  • Current Ratio
  • Debt-to-Equity Ratio
  • Return on Assets

Best-Performing Models:

  • Random Forest: 93% accuracy.
  • Neural Networks: 87% accuracy, high recall for bankruptcy detection.

Insights:

  • Ensemble models outperformed simpler algorithms.
  • Balanced datasets improved predictive performance.

Conclusion

  • Financial ratios are essential tools for bankruptcy prediction.
  • Machine learning models enhance prediction accuracy, offering early warning signals to stakeholders.
  • Integrating additional data sources and improving model transparency will further improve the field.
